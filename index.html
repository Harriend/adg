AI PING PONG GAME



























Instructions

  1. The game is controlled using your right wrist
  2. Move 4-5 feet away from your webcam
  3. Now press the start button
  4. A red dot will appear on your right hand
  5. the red dot means that the AI has detected your right wrist
  6. Move your wrist up to move your paddle up and move it down to move the paddle down

    7. Good Luck!
